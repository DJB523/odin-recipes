Spaghetti



Description

A classic italian dish for dinner that won't break the bank!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of angel hair pasta
  • 1 bag of meatballs
  • 1 box of 4 cheese garlic bread
  • parmesian cheese
  • 1-2 jars of your favorite pasta sauce

Steps

  1. bring a pot of water to a boil, add in a fair amount of salt to flavor the water
  2. place meatballs into airfryer for 6 minutes at 350 degrees fahrenheit
  3. pour pasta sauce into sauce pan and simmer
  4. place meatballs into sauce pan to simmer with sauce
  5. once water is boiling place your serving of angel hair pasta into water and cook until al-dente
  6. drain pasta noodles of all except a small amount of water
  7. once noodles are done place garlic bread into air fryer for 2 minutes or until golden brown
  8. mix sauce pan into noodles with the remaining bit of water and mix
  9. Plate your pasta and garlic bread and sprinkle on desired amount of parmesian cheese and enjoy!
