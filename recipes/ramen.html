Ramen



Description

A classic spin on a broke college kids goto meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of your favorite ramen!
  • 1 egg
  • 1 steam bag of frozen corn
  • green onion

Steps

  1. bring a pot of water to a boil, amount of water varies depending on pack of ramen being used.
  2. fill a bowl with icewater and place beside oven
  3. chop green onion finely and set aside
  4. make steam bag of corn in the microwave and set aside
  5. once water is boiling place room temperature egg into water for 4 minutes then remove and immediatly place into ice water
  6. place noodles into boiling water and cook until noodles fully soften
  7. remove from heat and mix in seasoning packet
  8. pour into bowl and carefully peel the soft boiled egg and cut in half placing in bowl, then place desired amount of corn and green onion in bowl and enjoy!
