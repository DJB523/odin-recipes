Pizza



Description

Low on time or funds for takeout? This is the recipe for you! All you need is 4 simple ingredients and you got yourself a nice dinner!

Ingredients

  • pre-made pizza dough
  • pizza sauce
  • shredded low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. bring dough to room temperature and place in a covered bowl for atleast 30 minutes to 2 hours prior to baking
  2. pre-heat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit and if using pizza pan place in oven until hot
  3. roll out your dough on a flat surface sprinkled with a light amount of flour or cornstarch to prevent sticking
  4. once dough is rolled out place a spoonfull of sauce in the center and swirl it from the center out. Repeat until only the crust remains unsauced. DO NOT OVER SAUCE
  5. place as much cheese over the sauce as you would like, followed by your pepperoni.
  6. place on pizza stone or a pizza pan with some cornstarch.
  7. remove when cheese is golden brown and bottom is stiff but not burnt
